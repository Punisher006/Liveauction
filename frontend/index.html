About Live Auction

Welcome to Live Auction, the premier peer-to-peer investment platform. Join us to trade shares and grow your wealth!

You can invest for 4 days, 8 days, or 12 days with a 30%, 60%, and 95% Return on Investments (ROI) respectively.

How It Works

  1. Create an account: Provide accurate Mpesa details.
  2. Understand auction times: Auction times are 7:00 AM, 12:00 PM, and 7:00 PM.
  3. Participate: Refresh the page when the timer reaches 00:00:00.
  4. Bid: Enter an amount (Minimum KES 500) and select an investment period.
  5. Check pairings: View Mpesa details under Bought Shares.
